Verify and Save a Dictionary

After you finish editing your dictionary, verify its contents and save it.

After saving, it is good to verify the changes you've made to the dictionary. As the dictionary becomes larger there is a higher chance that a pattern may be entered into multiple categories. This is called a "collision". Verifying helps dictionary writers fix these errors.
  1. To save, click the Menu Icon and click Save
  2. To verify, click the Menu Icon and click Verify
    The following items are presented in a separate dialog box
    • A list of single word collisions
    • A list of multi-word collisions
    • Undefined Word Classes
    • Duplicate Word Classes
  3. If collisions are detected, click the Repair button to start an automatic tool that resolves single and multi word collisions
    Undefined and Duplicate word classes must be fixed manually
Export your dictionary to a .DAT file to share with others while protecting your intellectual property.
