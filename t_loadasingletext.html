Load a Single Text

Upload a single text to be analyzed by DocuScope.

Before analyzing, a dictionary must be loaded in DocuScope. Learn to Load a Dictionary first.
DocuScope currently accepts .DOCX or .TXT files for analysis. To upload a file
  1. After loading a dictionary, in the menu click Analysis > Open File...
  2. Find your text file, highlight, and click Open
