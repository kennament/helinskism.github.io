Editing a Dictionary

Learn to edit your dictionary to create your own tool for analysis.

Note: .DAT dictionary files cannot be edited by DocuScope. To edit, you must have full access to the original dictionary folder that houses the .TXT files that form the dictionary.

In many cases, a domain-specifc dictionary is necessary to carry out analysis. DocuScope offers the ability for users to edit a dictionary they have access to in order to customize the rhetoical patterns used for analysis.

  • Add LATs to the Dictionary

  • Edit Cluster and Dimension Properties

  • Move a Dimension
    Move a dimension to another cluster.
  • Move an LAT

    After you finish editing your dictionary, verify its contents and save it.
