Categories of Analysis

DocuScope assesses meaning through both individual words and streams of words in sequence.

DocuScope takes a developmental / rhetorical approach to language. By this, we mean that meaning in language is derived through streams of words more than individual words. The context in which a word appears is a part of this meaning, and DocuScope seeks to capture these intricacies with three levels of patterns. They are
  • Clusters
  • Dimensions
  • Language Action Types
To sum up, we start with large streams of words with clusters, which filter into smaller streams of dimensions until we reach the smaller words and phrases with LATs. Learn about this organizational system, then Loading a Text to analyze.
  • Clusters
    Clusters are the highest level of rhetorical categorization.
  • Dimensions
    Dimensions are the second level of rhetorical categorization.
  • Language Action Types
    Language Action Types (LATs) are the smallest level of categorization.
