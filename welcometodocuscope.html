Welcome to DocuScope

DocuScope is a corpus-based text analysis tool that performs both quantitative and qualitative analyses of the rhetorical strategies used in texts.

DocuScope uses a dictionary that consists of more than 40 million unique patterns of English that are classified into over 100 rhetorical functions. The tool allows users to see rhetorical moves and linguistic patterns in texts to deepen the understanding behind an author's language choices. DocuScope also allows users to build their own customized dictionaries according to their own language theories to use in analysis.

Use DocuScope to:

  • Capture rhetorical patterns across a single text or a corpus
  • Filter for different rhetorical properties
  • Visualize these patterns

Learn how to use DocuScope by reading about the program, or dive right in and load a text to analyze.
